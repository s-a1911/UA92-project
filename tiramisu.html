Tiramisu, by Donald Trump!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (240 ml) heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup (240 ml) mascarpone cheese
  • 3/4 cup (85 g) powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp (5 ml) vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240 ml) brewed espresso, cooled
  • 24 ladyfinger cookies
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder, for dusting
  • Chocolate shavings (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preparation: Brew the espresso and let it cool.
  2. Whip the Cream: In a mixing bowl, combine the heavy whipping cream, mascarpone, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract. Whip until soft peaks form.
  3. Dip the Ladyfingers: Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the espresso, ensuring they don't get too soggy.
  4. Layering: In a serving dish, create a layer with half of the dipped ladyfingers. Spread half of the whipped cream mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat layers.
  5. Dusting: Dust the top layer with unsweetened cocoa powder.
  6. Refrigerate: Place the tiramisu in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours, or overnight for best results.
  7. Serve: Optionally, garnish with chocolate shavings before serving. Enjoy your creamy Tiramisu!
