Pain au Chocolat, by Barack Obama!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/4 cups (300 ml) warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup (50 grams) granulated sugar
  • 4 cups (500 grams) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup (115 grams) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • 8 ounces (225 grams) good-quality chocolate, preferably dark
  • 1/4 cup (50 grams) granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup (60 ml) heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a bowl, combine the warm milk, yeast, and 1 tablespoon of the sugar. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Mix the Dough:
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, remaining sugar, and salt. Pour in the yeast mixture and mix until it comes together.
    • Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface until it becomes smooth and elastic, about 5-10 minutes.
  3. Incorporate the Butter:
    • Flatten the dough and spread the softened butter over it. Fold the dough over the butter and knead until the butter is fully incorporated.
  4. First Rise:
    • Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Prepare the Chocolate Filling:
    • While the dough is rising, chop the chocolate into sticks or chunks.
    • In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream and sugar until the sugar is dissolved. Remove from heat and pour it over the chocolate. Stir until the chocolate is melted and smooth. Let it cool.
  6. Shape the Pastries:
    • Once the dough has doubled, roll it out into a large rectangle, about 1/4 inch thick.
    • Cut the dough into smaller rectangles, placing a piece of chocolate at one end of each rectangle.
    • Roll up the dough, enclosing the chocolate, and place the pastries on a baking sheet, seam side down.
  7. Second Rise:
    • Cover the pastries with a clean kitchen towel and let them rise for another 30-45 minutes.
  8. Bake:
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Brush the pastries with the beaten egg and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown.
