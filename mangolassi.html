Mango Lassi by Hilary Clinton!

    Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled, pitted and cubed
  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup cold milk
  • 4 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

    Instructions:

  1. Preparation:
    • Peel the mangoes, remove the seed, and chop them into small pieces.
  2. Blend:
    • In a blender, combine the chopped mangoes, yogurt, milk, sugar, and cardamom. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Consistency:
    • If the lassi is too thick, you can add more milk to reach your desired consistency.
  4. Serve:
    • Pour the mango lassi into glasses, add a couple of ice cubes if preferred, and serve chilled. Optionally, garnish with a pinch of cardamom or some chopped mango pieces.
