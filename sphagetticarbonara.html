Spaghetti Carbonara, by Joe Biden!

    Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • A small bunch of fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • Extra virgin olive oil

    Instructions:

  1. Prepare Pasta:
    • Boil spaghetti in a large pot of salted water until al dente. Save a cup of pasta water and then drain.
  2. Cook Pancetta:
    • While pasta is cooking, heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat, add the pancetta, and saut√© until it becomes crispy and golden.
  3. Prepare Sauce:
    • In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, pecorino, parmesan, and freshly ground black pepper.
  4. Combine Pasta:
    • Add drained spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta and toss to combine. Remove skillet from heat, and quickly pour in egg/cheese mixture, stirring quickly to create a creamy sauce using the reserved pasta water as needed.
  5. Finish & Serve:
    • Top with more cheese and parsley, and serve immediately!
