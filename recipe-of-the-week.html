Recipe of the Week

Quiche Lorraine, by a random bloke off YouTube!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pie crust
  • 6 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the pie crust in a pie dish and crimp the edges.
  3. Sprinkle the crumbled bacon, shredded Swiss cheese, and diced onion evenly over the pie crust.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, heavy cream, salt, pepper, and ground nutmeg (if using).
  5. Pour the egg mixture over the bacon, cheese, and onions in the pie crust.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes or until the quiche is set and the top is lightly browned.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
